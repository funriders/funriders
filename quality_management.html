Quality management is the backbone of safe, reliable, and enjoyable play environments. It ensures that every play area is designed, built, and maintained with the highest standards of safety, performance, and durability, creating spaces where children and families can enjoy worry-free experiences.

At Funriders, strict quality practices are followed at every stage, from design and manufacturing through installation and inspection, ensuring that every play system delivers superior safety, long-lasting durability, and complete peace of mind for users.

Safety, Trust & User Confidence

Everyone likes to watch their children strut and bring down the house when they leave them at a play center or theme park. For the parents to be at complete tranquility, the play center should be fashioned in such a way that the kids should be free from menace and have zero risk of stumbling blocks and harsh surfaces. Adults who visit theme parks and FECS should get an assured inner feeling that they would come out of the ride without a scratch even when it looks like a Herculean task to return alive. Therefore, QUALITY of the playgrounds and the equipment in it should be top notch and no compromise should be ever done on it, whatsoever be the reasons be.

Manufacturing Aligned with International Standards

Funriders, following the footprints of Buildindia, has teamed up with some of the best manufacturing firms to supply top-end play equipment having quality certifications with worldwide reception. Our products have been crafted by Industries adhering to international standards like ASTM, EN, 15 etc. in its work process.

Funriders Leisure and Amusement Pvt. Ltd. is an 150 9001 2008 certified company. 150 9001 is used to measure manufacturing standards and to certify company compliance with quality managerment systems covering design, development, production, installation , inspection and testing.

Industry & Government Recognition

Funriders is a member of Indian Association of Amusement Parks and Industries (IAAPI), the apex body representing the interests of Amusement Parks, Theme Parks, Water Parks and Family Entertainment Centre in India. IAAPI works to create and sustain an environment conducive to the development of Amusement sector in India, partnering industry, Government, and civil society. through advisory and consultative processes.

Funriders (Buildindia) is also a member of National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC), a Government of India enterprise under Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME). NSIC has been working to promote aid and foster the growth of micro, small and medium enterprises in the country.
